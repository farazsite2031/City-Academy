About Us

Why Families Choose City Academy

City Academy is dedicated to providing high-quality education with experienced teachers, modern teaching methods and a supportive learning environment.

Who We Are

An academy built by educators, for Matric and Intermediate students of Misrial Chowk and nearby Rawalpindi Cantt localities.

Our Mission

To deliver focused, result-oriented teaching so every student clears board exams and entry tests with confidence.

Our Vision

To be Rawalpindi's most trusted academy, known for discipline, quality notes and consistent board results.

Why Choose Us

Weekly and monthly tests, complete notes, past papers and one-on-one career guidance under one roof.
